Why PCA for satellite imagery?
Multispectral bands are often highly correlated (especially adjacent bands). PCA decorrelates them, concentrating most variance into the first 2–3 components. Common uses:
• Feature compression for classification (fewer inputs, less noise)
• Change detection — differences in PC space highlight change
• Visualisation — PC1/PC2/PC3 as RGB gives more contrast than any 3 raw bands
• Noise isolation — last PCs carry mostly sensor noise
PC1 ≈ Brightness, PC2 ≈ Greenness, PC3 ≈ Wetness (similar to Tasseled Cap transform).

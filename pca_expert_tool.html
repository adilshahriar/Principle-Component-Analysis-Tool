About PCA

PCA finds orthogonal directions (principal components) of maximum variance in your data. The first PC captures the most variance, each subsequent PC is uncorrelated with those before it. Use the Kaiser criterion (eigenvalue ≥ 1) or the elbow rule on the scree plot to decide how many components to keep. Standardise features when they have different units or scales.
